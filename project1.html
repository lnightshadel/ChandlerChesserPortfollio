Project Overview

My goal was to understand the nature of cyber attacks by intentionally exposing the VM to the public,
thereby inviting potential attackers. Here's a detailed breakdown of my experiment:


Setting Up the Azure Virtual Machine:

I initiated the experiment by creating a virtual machine on Microsoft Azure with a focus on low expense and system resource managment to acheive what I needed without incuring additional cost.

This VM served as the target for real cyber attacks in a controled and contained enviornment to simulate actual attacks. I deliberately disabled the system's firewall, leaving its ports exposed to the public internet. This allowed for port scanning by potential attackers, simulating real-world cybersecurity threats via attempts to remote connect to the system.

This attack showcases the power and persistence of brute-force attacking on a global scale with thousands of attempts from multiple countries automating their attacks until they gain connection.
